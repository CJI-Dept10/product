China Jiangsu International Chemical Co., Ltd. (CJI Chemical) is a core subsidiary of the state-owned CJI Group. Established in 1984, we have spent nearly four decades perfecting the international distribution of fine chemicals, pharmaceutical intermediates, and high-purity solvents.

Our operation is backed by AEO Advanced Certification, ensuring the fastest customs clearance for our global partners. We serve over 80 countries, providing integrated supply chain solutions that combine sourcing, QC, and multimodal logistics.
